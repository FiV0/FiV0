## Hi, I'm Finn 👋

I am building a Datalog engine called [Triplox](https://triplox.xyz) (planning to open-source) on top of [SlateDB](https://github.com/slatedb/slatedb). Triplox is very much inspired by [Datomic](https://www.datomic.com/). I experiment on features for Triplox on [hooray](https://github.com/FiV0/hooray2).

I am a big fan of [Datalog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Datalog), and in particular [EDN Datalog](https://v1-docs.xtdb.com/language-reference/1.24.3/datalog-queries/).
Previously I worked on [XTDB](https://github.com/xtdb/xtdb/), an immutable SQL database with time-travel support. We also explored bringing Datalog features to a relational engine with [XTQL](https://docs.xtdb.com/xtql/tutorials/introducing-xtql.html).

As SlateDB is the backbone of Triplox, I occasionally contribute to SlateDB, mostly on user-facing features.

Database-related musings land at [finnvolkel.com](https://finnvolkel.com) every now and then.
