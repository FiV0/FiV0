## Hi, I'm Finn 👋

I am currently working on a Datalog engine called [Triplox](https://triplox.xyz) (open-sourcing pretty soon) on top of SlateDB. Triplox is very much inspired by [Datomic](https://www.datomic.com/). I experiment on features for Triplox on [hooray](https://github.com/FiV0/hooray2).

I am a big fan of [Datalog](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Datalog) and in particular [EDN Datalog](https://v1-docs.xtdb.com/language-reference/1.24.3/datalog-queries/).
Previously I worked on [XTDB](https://github.com/xtdb/xtdb/), an immutable SQL database with time-travel support. We also tried to bring Datalog features to a relational engine with [XTQL](https://docs.xtdb.com/xtql/tutorials/introducing-xtql.html).

I occasionally contribute to [SlateDB](https://github.com/slatedb/slatedb), but mostly on user-facing features.

I sometimes blog about database related things at [finnvolkel.com](https://finnvolkel.com).
